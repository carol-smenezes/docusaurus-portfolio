# View Map in Mobile

Use the map in MC Kinetic to find assets and work orders, and to identify where technicians need to go.

## Prerequisites

The asset or work order must have a location with latitude and longitude coordinates to display on the map. Enter this information in one of the following ways:

- In the main application, enter the latitude and longitude in the **Asset details** page.
- In MC Kinetic, select a work order location in the map.

## Select a work order location

1. Open a work order workbench.
2. Find and open a work order.
3. Expand **Location**.
4. In the map view, tap **Edit location**.
5. To zoom in, pinch two or more fingers together and spread them apart.
6. To zoom out, pinch two or more fingers together on the screen.
7. Long-press a location on the map.
    
    The pin appears on the map.
    

## Open the map view

1. Open a work order workbench.
2. Tap **Map**.
    
    The map view opens.
    
3. To zoom in, pinch out or tap **Zoom in**.
4. To zoom out, pinch in or tap **Zoom out**.
5. Tap **My location** to center the map on your current location.
6. Tap a pin to view its associated **Work Order ID**.
