Sign in to Meridian Cloud

If you have a user account, sign in and start using Meridian Cloud. If you don't have an account yet, contact your system administrator to request access and sign up.

Browser compatibility​

Use the latest version of the following supported desktop browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Microsoft Edge
  • Mozilla Firefox

Sign in​

Sign in with a Google, Microsoft, or Azure AD account. Use one of the following authentication methods to sign in:

  • Google
  • Microsoft
  • Azure AD

You can also use a SAML Identity Provider if your system administrator enables SAML Authentication. Contact your system administrator for more information.

Sign in with Google​

Use a Google account to sign in to Meridian Cloud. You can create a Google account using your company email address.

  1. Open your Meridian Cloud tenant URL.
  2. In Sign in, click Google.
  3. In Sign in with Google, select your Google account.
  4. Enter your Google account credentials.

You're automatically signed in to Meridian Cloud. The Landing page displays the Meridian clients, where you can begin working with Meridian.

Sign in with Microsoft or Azure AD​

Use a Microsoft account to sign in to Meridian or contact your system administrator for instructions on signing in with Azure AD.

  1. Open your Meridian Cloud tenant URL.
  2. In Sign in, click Microsoft or Azure AD.
  3. On the Microsoft Sign in page, enter your email credentials.

You're automatically signed in to Meridian Cloud. The Landing page displays the Meridian clients, where you can begin working with Meridian.
